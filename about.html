About Us

The Shotline is a digital news platform created by Emma Makhoul, a university student passionate about journalism.

Our mission is to empower students by providing them with a platform for internship opportunities, breaking news, and sharing stories. The Shotline is where the next generation of journalists can start their journey in media.

With The Shotline, we aim to inspire creativity, foster new talent, and offer a space for aspiring journalists to showcase their work and make an impact.
